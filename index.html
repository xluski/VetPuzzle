VetPuzzle is a powerful bookmarklet that fetches answers, skips videos, auto-answers questions, and runs in stealth — all with zero installs.

Install Bookmarklet View on GitHub
What it does

Fetch Answers

Instantly retrieves all multiple choice answers for any Edpuzzle assignment.

Auto Answer

Automatically submits answers for all MC and open-ended questions using AI.

Skip Video

Jump to any point in the video instantly. No more waiting around.

Stealth Mode

Randomizes timing and behavior patterns to reduce detection risk.

Speed Control

Change playback speed up to 16×. Works without triggering auto-pause.

Canvas & Schoology

Works on Edpuzzles embedded in Canvas and Schoology — not just edpuzzle.com.

Install in seconds.

No extensions, no logins, no BS. Just drag a button.

1
Show your bookmarks bar — press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac)
2
Drag the button below into your bookmarks bar. On Firefox, right-click → "Bookmark Link"
3
Navigate to any Edpuzzle assignment and click the bookmarklet to launch VetPuzzle
